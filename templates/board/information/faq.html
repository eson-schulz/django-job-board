Frequently Asked Questions

General

How secure is Owatonna Jobs Online?

All connections to Owatonna Jobs Online are encrypted using https, protecting general traffic to it. The only confidential information that we store is employers' passwords, which are protected with PBKDF2 and salted in our database. All credit card transactions are handled and stored by Stripe, which was recently audited and certified to be a PCI Service Provider Level 1, the highest level of certification available.

You can read more about Stripe's security here.

How can I contact you with questions or comments?

You can find our email on our contact page.

Employers

Why should I upgrade to a paid account?

Paid account on Owatonna Jobs Online are given many features over the free accounts. One of the best reasons to upgrade is the allowance of more posts to the site.

Also, with paid accounts, your company logo is displayed next to your postings on the search page, on the post details page, and on your company page.

As you can see, the addition of your own company logo makes your posts pop more and become more attractive to job seekers. The company details page is similarity drawn as the job details page.

Along with a company logo, we include company social media links in all paid accounts. This means that you can attach your social media (Twitter, Facebook) presence to your job postings.

All paid posts also include a posting to our Facebook and Twitter pages. We will automatically post a link to your job the first time your post any jobs on our website.

To prevent spam, all free accounts must have their posts verified before they are posted. All paid accounts can instantly post their jobs with no delay.

What is a highlighted post?

Highlighted posts are put at in position one or two of any category. Along with that, a green outline is given to the posts to make them stand out even more.

Can I advertise something besides jobs?

Yes, we have several areas for advertisements on different pages. If you are interested, please contact us.
